About Me

Systematic curiosity meets strategic thinking

Philipp is a multidisciplinary problem solver and systems thinker who specializes in strategy and risk management, serving as a guiding compass for organizations seeking external insight on their most important projects.

With a proven track record across machine, automotive, health, and life-science industries, he has led top-tier teams as a systems engineer and secured multiple patents that drive innovation. His mission is to uncover customer needs and craft strategies that harness emerging technologies while proactively managing risk for sustainable success.
